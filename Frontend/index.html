About Sahyadri

Your Future Campus

Experience a campus like no other along the Mangalore-Bangalore National Highway 48, where Sahyadri College of Engineering and Management (SCEM) flourishes on the banks of the Nethravathi River. Amidst nature's pristine beauty, this sought-after abode of learning boasts excellent infrastructure and a faculty committed to shaping futures. Established in 2007 under the Bhandary Foundation, SCEM is a premier technological institution dedicated to quality education, recognized for its 'A' grade accreditation by NAAC, NBA approval for five engineering programs, and numerous accolades, making it a beacon of innovation, collaboration, and academic excellence in India.



PG Course

Master of Business Administration

The Department of Business Administration at Sahyadri College of Engineering & Management (SCEM) is a prestigious academic division that is affiliated with Visveswaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgavi. Established in 2008 under the Bhandary Foundation, the college is committed to providing high-quality education and fostering an environment of academic excellence. The MBA program at SCEM is a two-year full-time course that focuses on developing managerial skills and leadership qualities essential for success in the dynamic business world. With a comprehensive curriculum, the program aims to equip students with the necessary tools to excel in various industries, including finance, marketing, operations, and human resources.
